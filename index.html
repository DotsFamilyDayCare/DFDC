About Dots Family Daycare (FDC) - Flagstone

Dots FDC is run by a registered Educator Danielle Van Veen and is governed by the coordination unit Busy Butterflies Education and Care Service. Dots FDC opened in January 2019, and was founded by Danielle as a way to offer a loving home based learning environment to children while still being available for her own two children. At Dots FDC we can provide flexible arrangements with a minimum of 8 hours a day between 6:00am and 6:00pm, Monday to Friday. We cater to part time, Full time, casual, before and after school care and vacation care. The maximum number of children we may care for at any one time is seven, with no more than four children not yet at school. Pleas note- The Educator’s own children are included as part of this ratio. Our service is approved for Child Care Subsidy (CCS). CCS is a payment from the Australian Government that helps you with the cost of your child care.
